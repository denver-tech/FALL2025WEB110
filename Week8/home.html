The Great Gasby

Author: F. Scott Fitzgerald

The Great Gasby is a novel about the American Dream, wealth, and love in the Jazz Age. It tells the story of Jay Gatsby and his obsession with Daisy Buchanan.

Fun Fact: The novel was initialy a commercial failure and only became popular after Fitzgerald's death.
